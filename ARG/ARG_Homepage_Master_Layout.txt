ARG WEBSITE – HOMEPAGE MASTER LAYOUT (FOR CLAUDE AI IMPLEMENTATION)
===================================================================

OVERVIEW:
---------
This homepage is part of a disguised drug addiction awareness website. Its actual purpose is to serve as the gateway to an Alternate Reality Game (ARG). It must look fully functional, professional, and legitimate to a casual user. The ARG should be completely hidden unless a user follows specific clues (not discussed here). Below is the comprehensive structure, section by section.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. WEBSITE STRUCTURE
---------------------
- Site Width: Full-width responsive (100% width on all devices)
- Art Style: Clean, minimal, modern; medical/NGO theme
- Primary Colors: Soft white (#f8f9fa), calm blue (#5ca8e3), and muted grey (#d9d9d9)
- Font: "Inter", "Open Sans", or "Nunito Sans" (simple and readable)
- Icons: Flat icons with vector/SVG support

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. SITE HEADER (TOP SECTION)
-----------------------------
- Position: Fixed at the top of the page, stays as user scrolls
- Height: ~70px
- Logo (left side):
  > Name: “AwakeAid” (or similar NGO-like name)
  > Style: Minimal, flat design. Blue teardrop or awareness ribbon incorporated
  > Font: Bold sans-serif, lowercase preferred
  > Clickable to return to homepage

- Navigation Menu (right side of header):
  Links in a row (left to right):
  > Home
  > Awareness
  > Stories
  > Support
  > Resources
  > Contact
  > Language Toggle (optional, dropdown or flag icon)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. HERO SECTION (Main Visual Focus)
------------------------------------
- Height: 100vh (full screen)
- Background: Calm, soft-focus image of a sunset cityscape or silhouetted human figure (neutral, not specific to drugs)
- Overlay: Transparent dark layer (rgba(0,0,0,0.4)) for contrast
- Foreground Text:
  > Title: “Every Story Matters.”
    Font: Extra-bold, large (~60px), white
  > Subtext: “Join us in raising awareness and fighting stigma around substance abuse.”
    Font: Medium, ~24px, muted white (#f0f0f0)
  > CTA Button: “Read Their Stories” (links to Stories page)
    Style: Rounded button, soft blue background, hover = dark blue

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. “WHAT WE DO” SECTION
------------------------
- Position: Directly below Hero section
- Background: White
- Layout: Three horizontal cards (on desktop), stacked on mobile
- Each Card:
  > Icon: Flat style icon (e.g., hand-heart, awareness ribbon, group)
  > Title: Awareness / Support / Community
  > Text: 2-line description of services
- Style: Drop shadow, rounded corners, blue border hover effect

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. “DID YOU KNOW?” FACTS SECTION
---------------------------------
